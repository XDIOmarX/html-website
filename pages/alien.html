Alien (1979)

Director: Ridley Scott  |  Runtime: 1h 57m  |  Rating: 8.4 / 10

Synopsis

The commercial towing vessel Nostromo is diverted by a distress signal from an uncharted moon. A crew member returns carrying an alien organism that eventually kills its host and begins hunting the remaining crew one by one.

Warrant officer Ellen Ripley (Sigourney Weaver) emerges as the film's unlikely hero — a practical, methodical survivor who became one of cinema's greatest protagonists.

Key facts

Director Ridley Scott
Year 1979
Creature design H.R. Giger
Budget $11 million
Oscar win Best Visual Effects
Franchise movies 8 (and counting)

Why it matters

H.R. Giger's biomechanical Xenomorph design was so original it won the Oscar for visual effects and has never truly been replicated. Ripley also made history as one of the first female action heroes in blockbuster cinema.
