About this site

What this is

A multi-page HTML website to showcase science-fiction movies — specifically the movies that have most influenced the genre and left a lasting mark on popular culture.

What makes a "classic"?

For this list, a classic must do at least one of the following:

  • Invent or redefine a visual or narrative language that others copied.
  • Ask a philosophical question that lingers long after the credits roll.
  • Feature a performance, design, or score that stands apart from its era.

Every movie in this collection passes at least two of those tests.
